Who We Are

Grilling is an art.
You are the artist.

Chuchoose was born from a simple but radical idea: the best meal is the one you design yourself. We put the creative control in your hands — you choose the cuts, the glazes, the sear level, the sides.

Our kitchen is your canvas. Our grill is your brush. From perfectly charred ribeye skewers to slow-basted sirloin steaks with our signature Chuchoose Glaze, every plate is a one-of-a-kind masterpiece.

What sets us apart isn't just the food — it's the experience of crafting something truly yours.

Our Story

Born from a Nickname,
Built for Everyone.

Chuchoose started as a half-serious joke at a backyard cookout. The host — known to friends as "Chuchu" — kept insisting that every guest should season their own skewer, pick their own glaze, and take ownership of their plate. "You choose," he'd say. "Chuchu-choose it."

The name stuck. The concept grew into something bigger: a dining philosophy where the customer's preference isn't a detail but the entire point. No default orders, no standard plates — just you, the grill, and every combination imaginable.

What began as a pop-up stall at a local night market has grown into a full restaurant experience, but the heart of it remains the same: your meal, your way, every time.

2021 — THE BACKYARD
Chuchu's famous cookout philosophy goes viral among friends. The "you choose" concept is born over charcoal and cold drinks.
2022 — NIGHT MARKET POP-UP
First public appearance at a local night market. 200 skewers sold out in under two hours. Word spreads fast.
2023 — FIRST BRICK & MORTAR
Chuchoose opens its first dine-in location with 8 signature glazes, an open-flame grill station, and a line out the door from day one.
2024 — THE GLAZE LAB
We launch our in-house Glaze Lab, letting customers mix two glazes to create a personal signature. Over 1,000 unique combinations recorded to date.
TODAY — YOUR TABLE
The journey continues. New cuts, new glazes, same promise: every meal at Chuchoose is entirely, unapologetically yours.
